United World Colleges Scholarship

IB Diploma

UWC Scholarship is a fully funded program that allows high school students to study the IB Diploma at international boarding schools worldwide.

Country: Multiple countries including UK, Germany, Italy, Canada, USA

Study Level: Secondary School (Grades 11–12, IB Diploma)

Funding: Fully Funded

Duration: 2 years

Details: Covers tuition, boarding, meals, living expenses, insurance, materials, travel

Eligibility: International students 15–17 years old with good academics, leadership, English

Application: Open (varies by country)
